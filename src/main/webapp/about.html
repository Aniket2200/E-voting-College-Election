Audyogik Tantra Shikshan Sanstha's ( A.T.S.S. )

Institute of Industrial and Computer Management and Research

(Approved by AICTE & Affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University, Recognized by Govt. of Maharashtra,Accredited by NAAC)

IICMR ( Institute of Industrial and Computer Management and Research) is an AICTE, New Delhi, approved technical education institute. This Leading Institute of Computer Studies in Pune is affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University, recognized by the Government of Maharashtra, and Accredited by NAAC. It offers technical education, computer education, management courses, and Software Engineering course in Pune for the students. The intake for this course is 120 students. IICMR goes by the goal of innovation, implementation, and inspiration. The parent trust, namely ATSS, run it, which is headed by Mr. M.D. Jambhekar. The Director of the MCA department is Dr. Deepali Sawai, who is an experienced academician. The Institute teaches productive and innovative learning among the students because of its cutting-edge learning practices. Moreover, the teaching model includes practical and theoretical learning with project provision and brainstorming sessions. IICMR has also signed the MOU with leading industry groups to provide international certifications to its students. The Pre-Placement training sessions provided at the Institute helps the students to get placed into excellent companies. The faculty and staff at the IICMR possess an excellent experience of almost 14 years, which makes them impart innovative and modern learning practices. We strive our best to teach social responsibility and learning discipline among the students through sustainable practices programs.

About Us

Institute of Industrial and Computer Management and Research was established in the year 2007-08. Initially, it started functioning with Master Of Computer Application (MCA) ,offering 2 years duration Degree Programme in computer Engineering. A two years duration Masters Programme in Business Administration (MBA) was added in the year 2008-09.
